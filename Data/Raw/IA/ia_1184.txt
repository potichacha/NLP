Parsing: The process of analyzing the grammatical structure of a sentence. It involves identifying the subject, verb, and object of a sentence and their relationships with each other. Sentiment Analysis: The process of identifying the sentiment (positive, negative, or neutral) of a sentence or a piece of text. This is useful in understanding the overall tone of a text. Machine Translation: The process of translating text from one language to another. Text Generation: The process of generating text based on a given prompt or context. This can be used for various applications such as chatbots, automated content generation, and more.
