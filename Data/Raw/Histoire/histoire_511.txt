But Cordelia , disgusted with the flattery of her sisters , whose hearts she knew were far from their lips , and seeing that all their coaxing speeches were only intended to wheedle the old king out of his dominions , that they and their husbands might reign in his lifetime , made no other reply but this - that she loved his Majesty according to her duty , neither more nor less .
