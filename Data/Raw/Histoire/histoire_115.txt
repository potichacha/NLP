This Gonzalo was the same who had so kindly provided Prospero formerly with books and provisions , when his wicked brother left him , as he thought , to perish in an open boat in the sea . Grief and terror had so stupefied their senses that they did not know Prospero . He first discovered himself to the good old Gonzalo , calling him the preserver of his life ; and then his brother and the king knew that he was the injured Prospero .
