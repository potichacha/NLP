The prince , unmoved by the passionate exclamations of these women , on a careful examination of the facts pronounced his sentence , and by that sentence Romeo was banished from Verona . Heavy news to young Juliet , who had been but a few hours a bride and now by this decree seemed everlastingly divorced !
