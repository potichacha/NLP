She would fain have recalled her words , but that was impossible ; fain would she have stood upon form , and have kept her lover at a distance , as the custom of discreet ladies is , to frown and be perverse and give their suitors harsh denials at first ; to stand off , and affect a coyness or indifference where they most love , that their lovers may not think them too lightly or too easily won ; for the difficulty of attainment increases the value of the object .
