But great events were happening at this time , of which Imogen knew nothing ; for a war had suddenly broken out between the Roman Emperor Augustus Caesar and Cymbeline , the King of Britain ; and a Roman army had landed to invade Britain , and was advanced into the very forest over which Imogen was journeying .
