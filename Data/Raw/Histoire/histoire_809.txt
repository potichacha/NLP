Claudio . `` Be ready to die to-morrow , '' said Isabel . `` Death is a fearful thing , '' said Claudio . `` And shamed life a hateful , '' replied his sister . But the thoughts of death now overcame the constancy of Claudio 's temper , and terrors , such as the guilty only at their deaths do know , assailing him , he cried out : `` Sweet sister , let me live !
