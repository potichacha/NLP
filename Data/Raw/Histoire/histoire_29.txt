So I being kept in irons six days and nights, and falling sick of a fever, I grew very weak, insomuch that when I was set out to ease myself, (which was but once in twenty four hours) I could neither stand nor go: So the officers that let me out, called some of my country|men to carry me into the ship's head to ease myself, and bring me back again to the irons.
