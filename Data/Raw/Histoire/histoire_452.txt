Nerissa 's ring . CYMBELINE During the time of Augustus Caesar , Emperor of Rome , there reigned in England ( which was then called Britain ) a king whose name was Cymbeline . Cymbeline 's first wife died when his three children ( two sons and a daughter ) were very young .
