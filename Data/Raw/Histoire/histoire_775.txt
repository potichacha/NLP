When they were become a little calm , Antipholus of Ephesus offered the duke the ransom money for his father 's life ; but the duke freely pardoned Aegeon , and would not take the money . And the duke went with the abbess and her newly found husband and children into the convent , to hear this happy family discourse at leisure of the blessed ending of their adverse fortunes .
