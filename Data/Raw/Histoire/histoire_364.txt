Proteus resolved to go to the duke and disclose the whole to him . This false friend began his tale with many artful speeches to the duke , such as that by the laws of friendship he ought to conceal what he was going to reveal , but that the gracious favor the duke had shown him , and the duty he owed his grace , urged him to tell that which else no worldly good should draw from him .
