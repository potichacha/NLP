KING LEAR Lear , King of Britain , had three daughters : Goneril , wife to the Duke of Albany ; Regan , wife to the Duke of Cornwall ; and Cordelia , a young maid , for whose love the King of France and Duke of Burgundy were joint suitors , and were at this time making stay for that purpose in the court of Lear .
