Claudio implored forgiveness of the old man Leonato for the injury he had done his child ; and promised that , whatever penance Leonato would lay upon him for his fault in believing the false accusation against his betrothed wife , for her dear sake he would endure it . The penance Leonato enjoined him was to marry the next morning a cousin of Hero 's , who , he said , was now his heir , and in person very like Hero .
