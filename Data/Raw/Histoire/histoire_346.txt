And when Lucetta , offered the letter to Julia she would not receive it , and chid her maid for taking letters from Proteus , and ordered her to leave the room . But she so much wished to see what was written in the letter that she soon called in her maid again ; and when Lucetta returned she said , `` What o'clock is it ?
