Thenceforward he became a true penitent , and resolved , relinquishing his unjust dominion , to spend the remainder of his days in a religious house . The first act of his newly conceived penitence was to send a messenger to his brother ( as has been related ) to offer to restore to him his dukedom , which be had usurped so long , and with it the lands and revenues of his friends , the faithful followers of his adversity .
