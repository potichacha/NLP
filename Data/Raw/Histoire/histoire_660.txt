Helena begged the ring from off his finger as a token of his regard , and he gave it to her ; and in return for this ring , which it was of such importance to her to possess , she gave him another ring , which was one the king had made her a present of . Before it was light in the morning she sent Bertram away ; and he immediately set out on his journey toward his mother 's house .
