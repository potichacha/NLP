They all were attentive to hear what thing the page would ask for ; and Lucius , her master , said to her : `` I do not beg my life , good lad , but I know that is what you will ask for . '' `` No , no , alas ! '' said Imogen . `` I have other work in hand , good master . Your life I can not ask for . '' This seeming want of gratitude in the boy astonished the Roman general .
