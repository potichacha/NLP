NOT SO HAPPY , BUT MUCH HAPPIER ! and prophesied that though he should never reign , yet his sons after him should be kings in Scotland . They then turned into air and vanished ; by which the generals knew them to be the weird sisters , or witches . While they stood pondering on the strangeness of this adventure
