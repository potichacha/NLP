Romeo , after the fray , had taken refuge in Friar Lawrence 's cell , where he was first made acquainted with the prince 's sentence , which seemed to him far more terrible than death . To him it appeared there was no world out of Verona 's walls , no living out of the sight of Juliet . Heaven was there where Juliet lived , and all beyond was purgatory , torture , hell .
