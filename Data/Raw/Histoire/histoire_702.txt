Katharine the Shrew , but the obedient wife , said , `` Let us go forward , I pray , now we have come so far , and it shall be the sun , or moon , or what you please ; and if you please to call it a rush candle henceforth , I vow it shall be so for me . '' This he was resolved to prove , therefore he said again , `` I say it is the moon . '' `` I know it is the moon , '' replied Katharine . `` You lie .
