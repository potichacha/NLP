In this letter she informed her that she was so much grieved at having driven Bertram from his native country and his home , that to atone for her offense , she had undertaken a pilgrimage to the shrine of St. Jaques le Grand , and concluded with requesting the countess to inform her son that the wife he so hated had left his house forever .
