Oberon likewise took the ass 's head from off the clown , and left him to finish his nap with his own fool 's head upon his shoulders . Oberon and his Titania being now perfectly reconciled , he related to her the history of the lovers and their midnight quarrels , and she agreed to go with him and see the end of their adventures .
