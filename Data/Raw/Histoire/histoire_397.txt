Jew and the generous merchant Antonio . Whenever Antonio met Shylock on the Rialto , ( or Exchange ) he used to reproach him with his usuries and hard dealings , which the Jew would bear with seeming patience , while he secretly meditated revenge . Antonio was the kindest man that lived , the best conditioned , and had the most unwearied spirit in doing courtesies ; indeed , he was one in whom the ancient Roman honor more appeared than in any that drew breath in Italy .
