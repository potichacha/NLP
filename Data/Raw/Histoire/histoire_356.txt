She who had wrought this wondrous change in Valentine was the Lady Silvia , daughter of the Duke of Milan , and she also loved him ; but they concealed their love from the duke , because , although he showed much kindness for Valentine and invited him every day to his palace , yet he designed to marry his daughter to a young courtier whose name was Thurio .
