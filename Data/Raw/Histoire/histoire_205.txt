Florizel and Perdita , Camillo and the old shepherd , arrived in safety at the court of Leontes . Leontes , who still mourned his dead Hermione and his lost child , received Camillo with great kindness and gave a cordial welcome to Prince Florizel . But Perdita , whom Florizel introduced as his princess , seemed to engross all Leontes 's attention .
