TAMING OF THE SHREW Katharine , the Shrew , was the eldest daughter of Baptista , a rich gentleman of Padua . She was a lady of such an ungovernable spirit and fiery temper , such a loud-tongued scold , that she was known in Padua by no other name than Katharine the Shrew .
