In this desperate and irremediable state ( as it seemed ) of his affairs , the eyes of all men were suddenly surprised at a new and incredible luster which this setting sun put forth . Once more Lord Timon proclaimed a feast , to which he invited his accustomed guests - lords , ladies , all that was great or fashionable in Athens .
