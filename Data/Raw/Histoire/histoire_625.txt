'' Bertram 's absence filled her eyes with tears and her heart with sorrow ; for though she loved without hope , yet it was a pretty comfort to her to see him every hour , and Helena would sit and look upon his dark eye , his arched brow , and the curls of his fine hair till she seemed to draw his portrait on the tablet of her heart , that heart too capable of retaining the memory of every line in the features of that loved face . Gerard de Narbon
