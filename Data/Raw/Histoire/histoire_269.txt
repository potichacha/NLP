Claudio said to the lady in the mask : `` Give me your hand , before this holy friar . I am your husband , if you will marry me . '' `` And when I lived I was your other wife , '' said this unknown lady ; and , taking off her mask , she proved to be no niece ( as was pretended ) , but Leonato 's very daughter , the lady Hero herself .
