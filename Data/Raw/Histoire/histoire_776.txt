Dromios ' humble joy must not be forgotten ; they had their congratulations and greetings , too , and each Dromio pleasantly complimented his brother on his good looks , being well pleased to see his own person ( as in a glass ) show so handsome in his brother . Adriana had so well profited by the good counsel of her mother-in-law that she never after cherished unjust suspicions nor was jealous of her husband .
