He instantly came forward and confessed to Cymbeline the cruel sentence which he had enjoined Pisanio to execute upon the princess , exclaiming , wildly : `` O Imogen , my queen , my life , my wife ! O Imogen , Imogen , Imogen ! '' Imogen could not see her beloved husband in this distress without discovering herself , to the unutterable joy of Posthumus , who was thus relieved from a weight of guilt and woe , and restored to the good graces of the dear lady he had so cruelly treated
