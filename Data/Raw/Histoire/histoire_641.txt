Bertram was married to Helena , a forced and uneasy marriage to Bertram , and of no promising hope to the poor lady , who , though she gained the noble husband she had hazarded her life to obtain , seemed to have won but a splendid blank , her husband 's love not being a gift in the power of the King of France to bestow .
