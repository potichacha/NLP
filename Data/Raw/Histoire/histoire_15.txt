Then the Captain sent for me upon the quarter-deck, and asked me, Why I refused to fight for the King, and why I re|fused to eat of his victuals? I told him, I was afraid to offend God, for my warfare was Spiri|tual, therefore I durst not fight with carnal wea|pons.
