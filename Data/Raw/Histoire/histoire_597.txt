With these miserable thoughts they found no peace , and Macbeth determined once more to seek out the weird sisters and know from them the worst . He sought them in a cave upon the heath , where they , who knew by foresight of his coming , were engaged in preparing their dreadful charms by which they conjured up infernal spirits to reveal to them futurity .
