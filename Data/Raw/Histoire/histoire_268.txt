But his heart was very sorrowful , and he passed that night in tears and in remorseful grief at the tomb which Leonato had erected for Hero . When the morning came the prince accompanied Claudio to the church , where the good friar and Leonato and his niece were already assembled , to celebrate a second nuptial ; and Leonato presented to Claudio his promised bride .
