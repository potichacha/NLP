'' `` When you are gentle , '' replied Petruchio , `` you shall have one , too , and not till then . '' The meat Katharine had eaten had a little revived her fallen spirits , and she said : `` Why , sir , I trust I may have leave to speak , and speak I will . I am no child , no babe . Your betters have endured to hear me say my mind ; and if you can not , you had better stop your ears . ''
