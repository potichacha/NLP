So these two wicked daughters , as if they strove to exceed each other in cruelty to their old father , who had been so good to them , by little and little would have abated him of all his train , all respect ( little enough for him that once commanded a kingdom ) which was left him to show that he had once been a king ! Not that a splendid train is essential to happiness , but from a king to a beggar is a hard change , from commanding millions
