Wednesday night Juliet drank off the potion . She had many misgivings lest the friar , to avoid the blame which might be imputed to him for marrying her to Romeo , had given her poison ; but then he was always known for a holy man . Then lest she should awake before the time that Romeo was to come for her ; whether the terror of the place , a vault full of dead Capulets ' bones , and where Tybalt , all bloody , lay festering in his shroud , would not be enough to drive her distracted .
