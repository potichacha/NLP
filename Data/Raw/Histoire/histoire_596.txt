But Macbeth continued to see the ghost , and gave no heed to all they could say , while he addressed it with distracted words , yet so significant that his queen , fearing the dreadful secret would be disclosed , in great haste dismissed the guests , excusing the infirmity of Macbeth as disorder he was often troubled with . To such dreadful fancies Macbeth was subject . His queen and he had their sleeps afflicted with terrible dreams , and the blood of Banquo troubled them not more
