It happened at this time that the king , who out of his royal condescension would oftentimes visit his principal nobility upon gracious terms , came to Macbeth 's house , attended by his two sons , Malcolm and Donalbain , and a numerous train of thanes and attendants , the more to honor Macbeth for the triumphal success of his wars .
