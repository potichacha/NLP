Ganymede that the Lady Rosalind had not yet made herself known to the duke her father , whose place of resort in the forest they had learned from Orlando . Ganymede met the duke one day , and had some talk with him , and the duke asked of what parentage he came . Ganymede answered that he came of as good parentage as he did , which made the duke smile , for he did not suspect the pretty shepherd-boy came of royal lineage .
