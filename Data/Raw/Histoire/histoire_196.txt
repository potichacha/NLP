Some lads and lasses were dancing on the green before the house , while others of the young men were buying ribands , gloves , and such toys of a peddler at the door . While this busy scene was going forward Florizel and Perdita sat quietly in a retired corner , seemingly more pleased with the conversation of each other than desirous of engaging in the sports and silly amusements of those around them .
