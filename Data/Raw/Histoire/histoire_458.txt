Imogen gave her husband a diamond ring which had been her mother 's , and Posthumus promised never to part with the ring ; and he fastened a bracelet on the arm of his wife , which he begged she would preserve with great care , as a token of his love ; they then bade each other farewell , with many vows of everlasting love and fidelity . Imogen remained a solitary and dejected lady in her father 's court , and Posthumus arrived at Rome , the place he had chosen for his banishment .
