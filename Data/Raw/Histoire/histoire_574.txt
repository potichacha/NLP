there arrived certain messengers from the king , who were empowered by him to confer upon Macbeth the dignity of Thane of Cawdor . An event so miraculously corresponding with the prediction of the witches astonished Macbeth , and he stood wrapped in amazement , unable to make reply to the messengers ; and in that point of time swelling hopes arose in his mind that the prediction of the third witch might in like manner have its accomplishment , and that he should one day reign king in Scotland .
