But Timon the naked , Timon the man-hater , was no longer Lord Timon , the lord of bounty , the flower of valor , their defense in war , their ornament in peace . If Alcibiades killed his countrymen , Timon cared not . If he sacked fair Athens , and slew her old men and her infants , Timon would rejoice .
