But now with music and state the banquet of smoking dishes was served up ; and when the guests had a little done admiring from where the bankrupt Timon could find means to furnish so costly a feast , some doubting whether the scene which they saw was real , as scarce trusting their own eyes , at a signal given the dishes were uncovered and Timon 's drift appeared .
