Then the duke left them together , and desired the provost who had the charge of the prisoners to place him where he might overhear their conversation . `` Now , sister , what is the comfort ? '' said Claudio . Isabel told him he must prepare for death on the morrow . `` Is there no remedy ? '' said Claudio . `` Yes , brother , '' replied Isabel , `` there is ; but such a one as if you consented to it would strip your honor from you and leave you naked . ''
