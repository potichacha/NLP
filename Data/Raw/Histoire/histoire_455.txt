Cymbeline , pitying the helpless state of this orphan , took Posthumus ( Cymbeline having given him that name because he was born after his father 's death ) , and educated him in his own court . Imogen and Posthumus were both taught by the same masters , and were playfellows from their infancy ; they loved each other tenderly when they were children , and , their affection continuing to increase with their years , when they grew up they privately married .
