noble Orsino , forsaking the sports of the field and all manly exercises in which he used to delight , passed his hours in ignoble sloth , listening to the effeminate sounds of soft music , gentle airs , and passionate love-songs ; and neglecting the company of the wise and learned lords with whom he used to associate , he was now all day long conversing with young Cesario . Unmeet companion no doubt his grave courtiers thought Cesario was for their once noble master , the great Duke Orsino .
