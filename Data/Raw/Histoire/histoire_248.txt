'' When Don John brought them near Hero 's chamber that night , they saw Borachio standing under the window , and they saw Margaret looking out of Hero 's window and heard her talking with Borachio ; and Margaret being dressed in the same clothes they had seen Hero wear , the prince and Claudio believed it was the lady Hero herself . Nothing could equal the anger of Claudio when he had made ( as be thought ) this discovery . All his love for the innocent Hero was at once
