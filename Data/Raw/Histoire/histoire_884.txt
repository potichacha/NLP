Antonio conceived such a friendship for Sebastian that he resolved to accompany him whithersoever he went ; and when the youth expressed a curiosity to visit Orsino 's court , Antonio , rather than part from him , came to Illyria , though he knew , if his person should be known there , his life would be in danger , because in a sea-fight he had once dangerously wounded the Duke Orsino 's nephew .
