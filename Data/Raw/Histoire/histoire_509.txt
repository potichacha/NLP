Regan , who was made of the same hollow metal as her sister , was not a whit behind in her professions , but rather declared that what her sister had spoken came short of the love which she professed to bear for his Highness ; in so much that she found all other joys dead in comparison with the pleasure which she took in the love of her dear king and father .
