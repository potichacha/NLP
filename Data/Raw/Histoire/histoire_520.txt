The honest freedom of this good Earl of Kent only stirred up the king 's wrath the more , and , like a frantic patient who kills his physician and loves his mortal disease , he banished this true servant , and allotted him but five days to make his preparations for departure ; but if on the sixth his hated person was found within the realm of Britain , that moment was to be his death .
