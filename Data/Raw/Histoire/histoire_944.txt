This was all the answer he vouchsafed to the weeping , disappointed senators ; only at parting he bade them commend him to his countrymen , and tell them that to ease them of their griefs and anxieties , and to prevent the consequences of fierce Alcibiades 's wrath , there was yet a way left , which he would teach them , for he had yet so much affection left for his dear countrymen as to be willing to do them a kindness before his death .
