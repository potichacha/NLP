Cymbeline , almost as much overwhelmed as he with joy , at finding his lost daughter so strangely recovered , received her to her former place in his fatherly affection , and not only gave her husband Posthumus his life , but consented to acknowledge him for his son-in-law . Bellarius chose this time of joy and reconciliation to make his confession . He presented Polydore and Cadwal to the king , telling him they were his two lost sons , Guiderius and Arviragus .
