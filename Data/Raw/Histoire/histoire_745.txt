The moment Antipholus of Syracuse had left the house he was met by a goldsmith , who , mistaking him , as Adriana had done , for Antipholus of Ephesus , gave him a gold chain , calling him by his name ; and when Antipholus would have refused the chain , saying it did not belong to him , the goldsmith replied he made it by his own orders , and went away ,
