So preposterous a disposal of his kingdom , so little guided by reason , and so much by passion , filled all his courtiers with astonishment and sorrow ; but none of them had the courage to interpose between this incensed king and his wrath , except the Earl of Kent , who was beginning to speak a good word for Cordelia , when the passionate Lear on pain of death commanded him to desist ; but the good Kent was not so to be repelled .
