But while he thus outwent the very heart of kindness , and poured out his bounty , as if Plutus , the god of gold , had been but his steward ; while thus he proceeded without care or stop , so senseless of expense that he would neither inquire how he could maintain it nor cease his wild flow of riot - his riches , which were not infinite , must needs melt away before a prodigality which knew no limits .
