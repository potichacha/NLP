But then her love for Romeo and her aversion for Paris returned , and she desperately swallowed the draught and became insensible . When young Paris came early in the morning with music to awaken his bride , instead of a living Juliet her chamber presented the dreary spectacle of a lifeless corse . What death to his hopes !
