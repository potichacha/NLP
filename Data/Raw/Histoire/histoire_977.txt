Mercutio , who had as much fire and youthful blood in him as Tybalt , replied to this accusation with some sharpness ; and in spite of all Benvolio could say to moderate their wrath a quarrel was beginning when , Romeo himself passing that way , the fierce Tybalt turned from Mercutio to Romeo , and gave him the disgraceful appellation of villain .
