Write a eulogy for Steve Martin

Ladies and gentlemen, family and friends, distinguished guests, we gather today to celebrate the life and legacy of an extraordinary individual, Steve Martin. As we remember him, we honor not only his remarkable career as a comedian, actor, musician, and writer, but also the indelible impact he left on each of us. Steve Martin was a man of immense talent, a true Renaissance man. From his early days as a stand-up comedian to his illustrious film career, Steve brought laughter and joy to countless people around the world. With his signature wit and razor-sharp humor, he had the rare ability to make us laugh, think, and feel all at once. Beyond his skill as a comedian, Steve was a gifted actor who graced the silver screen in a wide range of roles. In films like "The Jerk," "Planes, Trains, and Automobiles," "Father of the Bride," and "Roxanne," he endeared himself to audiences with his unique charm and vulnerability.
