what do psychotherapists charge on average

The cost of psychotherapy can vary widely depending on factors such as location, therapist experience, and type of therapy being offered. On average, psychotherapists in the United States may charge anywhere from $100 to $250 per session, with the national average hovering around $150 to $200 per session. However, some therapists may charge significantly more or less than this range. It's also worth noting that many therapists offer sliding scale fees, which means they adjust their rates based on a client's income or financial situation. Additionally, some insurance plans may cover some or all of the cost of therapy, which can help make it more affordable for individuals seeking treatment.

what is starting salary for a masters level CS grad

The starting salary for a master's level Computer Science (CS) graduate can vary depending on a number of factors such as location, industry, job role, and level of experience. In the United States, according to data from the National Association of Colleges and Employers (NACE), the average starting salary for a master's degree in computer science in 2021 was $90,769 per year. However, this figure can vary based on the factors mentioned above, as well as the particular company or organization that the graduate is employed by. In addition, salaries for CS graduates tend to be higher in certain regions such as Silicon Valley, where the cost of living is generally higher. It's also worth noting that experience and skill level can have a significant impact on salary, so those with more experience and advanced skill sets may earn higher salaries than entry-level graduates.
