Tell me the story of Henry Ford

Henry Ford was an American industrialist, inventor, and pioneer in the automotive industry. Born on July 30, 1863, in a small farming community in Dearborn, Michigan, Ford was the eldest of six children to parents William and Mary Ford. He grew up in a close-knit family that valued hard work, innovation, and self-reliance. From an early age, Ford showed a keen interest in machines and mechanics. At the age of 16, he left home to work as an apprentice machinist at a shipbuilding firm in Detroit. After completing his apprenticeship, he continued to work in various machine shops and factories, eventually returning to his family\
