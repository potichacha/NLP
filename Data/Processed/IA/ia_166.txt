What are some of the more interesting critiques of his Schank's Conceptual Dependency Theory?

Some interesting critiques of Schank's Conceptual Dependency Theory include: Lack of empirical evidence: The theory is based on anecdotal evidence and lacks empirical support. Overemphasis on syntax: The theory focuses heavily on the syntax of language and does not adequately account for the semantics or meaning of language. Failure to account for context: The theory does not adequately account for the role of context in language understanding and production. Inability to handle ambiguity: The theory struggles to handle the ambiguity that is inherent in natural language. Oversimplification of language: The theory oversimplifies the complexity of language and the cognitive processes involved in language understanding and production.
