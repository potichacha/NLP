what is the pledge of allegiance

The Pledge of Allegiance is a promise or oath of loyalty to the United States of America. It is typically recited or spoken aloud in various settings, including schools, government meetings, and patriotic events. The most common version of the Pledge of Allegiance is: "I pledge allegiance to the Flag of the United States of America, and to the Republic for which it stands, one Nation under God, indivisible, with liberty and justice for all."
