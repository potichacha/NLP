pie

Pie is a baked dish typically made with a pastry crust and filled with sweet or savory ingredients. The filling can be made with various fruits, such as apple, cherry, or blueberry, or with ingredients such as pumpkin, pecans, or chocolate. Pies can be served as a dessert or as a main course depending on the filling. They are a popular food item around the world and can be found in many different variations and styles.
