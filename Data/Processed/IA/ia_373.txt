How do I get good relative pitch like TheDooo?

Relative pitch is the ability to identify and reproduce musical notes and intervals without the aid of a reference tone. TheDooo is a talented musician known for his exceptional relative pitch skills, and developing such skills takes time and consistent practice. Here are some tips to help you improve your relative pitch: Train your ear: Listen to music and try to identify notes and intervals. You can start by listening to simple melodies and gradually move on to more complex pieces. Use technology: There are several apps and software programs available that can help you improve your relative pitch. These tools can generate random notes and intervals for you to identify, and they can also provide feedback to help you improve. Practice regularly: Consistency is key when it comes to developing relative pitch skills. Practice every day, even if it's just for a few minutes. Sing: Singing is a great way to develop your relative pitch skills. Try to sing along with your favorite songs and pay attention to the notes and intervals. Take lessons: Consider taking music lessons with a qualified teacher who can help you develop your relative pitch skills. A teacher can provide personalized feedback and help you improve more quickly. Remember that developing relative pitch takes time and effort, but with consistent practice and dedication, you can improve your skills over time.
