Act anent the vintners and baxters


ACT Anent the Vintners and Baxters.These are ordaining all Vintners with∣in this City, or Suburbs, to sell the Pint of their Mum-Beer for ten shillings Scots the Pint, after Saturday next, being this ninth of May instant; And whosoever shall contraveen or take any more, shall be lyable in an Fine of twenty Rex-dollars toties quoties. As also, that all Bread sold by Baxters, and Vintners, be conform to the Standard, both as to the VVeight and Fineness; under the like Penalty.
Extracted by me Jo. RICHARDSON.Edinburgh, Printed by the Heir of Andrew Anderson, Prin∣ter to His most Sacred Majesty, City and Colledge, Anno Dom. 1685.
