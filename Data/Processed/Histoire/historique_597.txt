Die Martis 29. Februarii. 1647.Resolved by the Lords and Commons assembled in Parliament.THat no person or persons whatsoever, presume to Raise, List, Muster, or gather together any Souldiers, or Forces upon pretence of Transporting them for the service of any forraigne Prince or State, within the City of London, or within ten miles of the said City.
